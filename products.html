Japfa is a specialty feed company in animal nutrition technology and is the pioneer to put in extensive research into FCR and derive a standard for gauging the Quality of Feed. Our Feed has a highly balanced and dynamic nutritional approach suitable for diversified climatic conditions with superior quality control.

FCR, the Feed Conversion Ratio plays a very important role in deciding the feed quality due to the factors like Health, Mortality, Bio-security which are dependent on FCR. Japfa understands the value of feed for its stakeholders and has been constantly maintaining the quality of its Feed.

Japfa was the first to introduce the pre-starter concept in India and started their offerings based on stage-wise nutrition with Breed specific solutions. The stages include Pre-starter, Starter & Finisher.

Pre-starter

Our Pre-Starter feed is fine crumbles containing all the nutrients required for early growth of the bird's life. It is a separate nutritional formula with highly digestible ingredients and balanced digestible amino acids, minerals, vitamins etc, produced after highly uniform mixing to assure minimum 180 grams at 7 days.

Starter

Japfa starter is a coarse crumble designed for feeding birds after 14 days. It is a product in continuation of our premium quality Pre-Starter, that contains balanced amino acids, minerals, vitamins, growth promoters, anticoccidial for optimum energy & protein, assuring minimum 1300 grams body weight at 28 days.

Finisher

It is a grow-out feed with high energy & balanced nutrition fed till marketing of birds. It contains all the nutrients required for fattening & assures high meat yield & less shrinkage at the market. A suitable combination of anticoccidial & growth promoters added in to prevent stress & improve bird’s resistance to infections.

Breed specific solutions

Japfa believes in stage-wise nutrition based on the life stages of the bird. We recognise that different breeds have different needs, that is why we offer wide variety of Feed Solutions specific to Poultry breed. Our products deliver precise nutrition for every bird, every breed, Always.

Broiler Feed
Feed engineered under optimum conditions and standard processes for the Broiler stock. A complete Nutrition solution in itself, for overall growth.
Layer Feed
Feed engineered under optimum conditions and standard processes for the Layer stock. A complete Nutrition solution in itself, for faster and standard grade quality eggs laid by the layer.
Breeder Feed
Feed engineered under optimum conditions and standard processes for the Layer stock. A complete Nutrition solution in itself, for increased productivity and no hindrance in their genetic engineering.
Concentrate

Concentrate is the add-on which acts as an additional source of protein-rich, nutritive diet, to be consumed as an additive to the farmer’s own feed.
