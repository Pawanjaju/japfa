Our Brands

A nutritious Feed plays a vital role in the growth of the bird, ensuring complete development and maximum weight gain by the time of sale. With the increasing demand for chicken maintenance efficiency, the improvement of sustainable animal feed specifications accompanied by an appropriate feeding program is increasingly inevitable. Japfa understands the importance of a balanced feed and hence introduced Brands that offer Breed specific solutions in Feed that help to maintain a better FCR.

Japfa’s Poultry Feed has been curated to provide the optimum combination of nutrients produced under well-monitored conditions by state-of-the-art technology and aided by expert bio-engineering in our endeavour to Make Good, Always.Japfa feeds, known for benchmarking broiler performance standards, high-quality feed for total nourishment and overall growth of the chick in a short duration, are produced in coherence with innovation and continuous R&D to always outdo themselves. Our Brands include high quality & consistent feeds in various sectors of poultry including Broiler, Layer and Breeder Feeds.

With an intent to produce most nutritious, value-driven and best outcome-oriented feed, specific to life phases of the bird, Japfa has introduced its feed specific brands- Comfeed, Benfeed, Ultrafeed.
