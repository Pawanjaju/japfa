Overview

Japfa is a leading Total Poultry Solution provider in India, since its inception in the year 1975 in Indonesia, our family has grown in leaps and bounds. We employ over 34,000 people across an integrated network of modern farming, processing and distribution facilities in Indonesia, China, Vietnam, India and Myanmar. We specialize in producing quality dairy, protein staples (poultry, beef, swine & aquaculture) and packaged food that nourish millions of people across the entire value chain.

Our first poultry feed mill was set up in Surabaya, Indonesia followed by establishing poultry breeding and aquaculture operations in quick succession. By 1989 PT Japfa was listed on the Jakarta Stock Exchange. Japfa Ltd was listed on the Singapore Stock Exchange by 2014 along-with having had introduced dairy, beef and pork products in both the Indonesian and Chinese markets. Japfa India was established in 1995 and offers Poultry & Integration along with Feed solutions.

Japfa has strongly believed in nurturing of mutually rewarding relationships based on trust and integrity. As pioneers of developing the best farm practices, we believe in delivering only the very best by providing excellent FCR, Breed Specific Solutions, and Precise Nutrition leading to healthy bird with good nutritious meat.

India’s poultry industry underwent a transformation due to various standards set by Japfa. At Japfa we believe it to be our responsibility to integrate with our partner farmers and aid their development, through hands-on technical assistance, complete feed solutions and guidance. Growing Towards Mutual Prosperity is the vision which we practise and uphold with Japfa’s various stakeholder groups.

Japfa’s ideology is to “Make Good. Always.” We consider it our duty to ensure that everyone gets access to the goodness of health and nutrition. To aid this, we are constantly improving ourselves with technology and research. Our zeal to provide only the very best gives us an edge over our competitors.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between science and sustainability by acting as liaisons between better yielding poultry farms and partner farmers. By sharing our expertise, providing constant technical support and exploring the endless possibilities to cater the ever-growing demand of each region we serve. With our immense R&D and innovation, we are not only pursuing greater productivity, profitability and sustainability for every poultry farm but also, working alongside producers and agro-vets to build the future of poultry farming.

To be the leading dependable provider of affordable protein foods in Asia by building on the foundation of our excellent teamwork and proven experience for the benefit of all stakeholders.
