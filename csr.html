Japfa 4 Kids

Japfa understands its moral responsibility and the importance of giving back to the society we live in. “Japfa4Kids” is our flagship CSR program dedicated to the well-being of India’s rural children.

Since 2008, the program has benefited over 85,000 rural children from over 450 schools in 19 provinces. Every year, “Japfa4Kids” hosts regional and national talent competitions amongst participating schools to showcase how sound nutrition and knowledge can help rural children realize their true potential. Each child is given nutritional support in the form of protein snacks, for up to a year along with personal health coaching to equip them with the knowledge to maintain good health.

Japfa Comfeed Indonesia began this initiative in 2008, which has won the Asia Responsible Entrepreneurship Award in the Health Promotion Category for 2017. Asia Responsible Entrepreneurship Awards (AREA) recognizes and honours Asian businesses for championing sustainable and responsible entrepreneurship in Health Promotion. The award emphasizes program innovation, solution, sustainability, integration and impact.

Japfa strongly believes that children are the true building blocks of our tomorrow and deserve the optimum nutrition and care to hone the best out of each child.
